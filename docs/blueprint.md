# **App Name**: NutriEase

## Core Features:

- Personalized Meal Reminders: Send meal reminders based on user's daily schedule and preferences.
- Healthy Meal Recommendations: Suggest nutritious options from nearby restaurants using geolocation and integrated data from food delivery apps like Shopee, Grabfood, and Gojek.
- Reduce Decision Fatigue Tool: Curate meal suggestions using an AI tool, filtering through delivery options to help users make better food choices with ease, with special attention to prices available in Shopee, Grabfood, and Gojek.
- Custom Diet Plans: Offer personalized meal recommendations for users with special dietary needs, suggesting simple recipes with available ingredients.
- Fitness Tracker Integration: Sync with fitness apps to tailor nutrition advice based on usersâ€™ physical activity, helping them meet their daily caloric and nutritional needs.
- Cheapest Harga: Incorporate prices from Shopee, Grabfood and Gojek in suggested food items

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Fresh lime green (#C0FF33), symbolizing health and vitality.
- Background color: Light, desaturated green (#E5FFCC).
- Accent color: Muted yellow (#E6DB74) for calls to action and highlights.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' for a modern, clean and objective feel.
- Use clean, minimalist icons representing different food groups and dietary preferences.
- Subtle animations to confirm selections.
